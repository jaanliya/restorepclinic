Machine 01

Super Inductive System

The Super Inductive System (SIS) uses high-intensity electromagnetic fields to stimulate muscle contraction, modulate pain, and promote neuromuscular re-education. It is one of the most powerful non-invasive therapy modalities available in physiotherapy.

The Inductura High Energy Inductive System generates precise electromagnetic pulses that penetrate deeply into tissues, making it highly effective for acute and chronic pain conditions.

  • Powerful pain modulation without direct skin contact
  • Stimulates involuntary muscle contractions for strengthening
  • Accelerates bone fracture healing
  • Effective for spasticity reduction in neuro patients

Machine 02

Shockwave Therapy

Extracorporeal Shockwave Therapy (ESWT) uses high-energy acoustic waves to break down calcifications, stimulate tissue healing, and provide powerful pain relief for chronic conditions. Our Shockwave Pro-1000 delivers focused acoustic energy with precision.

This is particularly effective for conditions that haven't responded to conventional physiotherapy, making it an excellent option for chronic tendinopathies and plantar fasciitis.

  • Breaks up calcium deposits in tendons
  • Stimulates new blood vessel formation
  • Long-lasting pain relief — often after just 3–5 sessions
  • Effective for chronic conditions unresponsive to other therapy
Plantar FasciitisTennis ElbowCalcific TendinitisAchilles TendinopathyShoulder Pain

Robotic Spinal Decompression

Computer-controlled spinal decompression therapy precisely relieves intradiscal pressure, reducing pain from disc herniations, nerve compressions, and degenerative disc disease. This is a highly specialised treatment that was previously only available in major metropolitan hospitals.

The robotic system adjusts in real time based on patient feedback, ensuring maximum therapeutic benefit while maintaining complete safety and comfort throughout the session.

  • Reduces intradiscal pressure to promote disc rehydration
  • Computer-controlled precision for maximum safety
  • Non-surgical alternative for disc herniation
  • Effective for both cervical and lumbar conditions
Disc HerniationPIVDSciaticaCervical RadiculopathyDegenerative Disc

Combination Therapy

Combination therapy simultaneously applies ultrasound and electrical stimulation (TENS/IFT) to targeted areas, producing synergistic therapeutic effects that are significantly greater than either modality used alone.

This approach is particularly effective for acute injuries, inflammation management, and conditions requiring both deep tissue treatment and pain relief simultaneously.

  • Simultaneous ultrasound and electrical stimulation
  • Superior anti-inflammatory effects
  • Accelerates soft tissue healing
  • Reduces muscle spasm and relieves pain rapidly
Soft Tissue InjuriesInflammationMuscle SpasmJoint PainSports Injuries

Class IV Laser Therapy

The Photon Class IV Laser delivers therapeutic laser energy deep into tissue — stimulating cellular repair processes, reducing inflammation, and providing powerful pain relief through photobiomodulation. Class IV lasers are more powerful than standard cold lasers, enabling treatment of deep-seated conditions.

Photobiomodulation triggers mitochondrial activity, accelerating ATP production and enhancing the body's natural healing mechanisms at a cellular level.

  • Deep tissue penetration for greater therapeutic effect
  • Accelerates wound and tissue healing
  • Powerful anti-inflammatory action
  • Effective for neuropathy and nerve regeneration
NeuropathyWound HealingArthritisDisc ConditionsNerve Injuries
Machine 06

Tecar Therapy (I-Tecar)

Tecar (Transfer of Energy Capacitive and Resistive) therapy uses radiofrequency energy to generate heat within deep tissues, stimulating metabolic activity, increasing local blood circulation, and promoting rapid recovery. The Biotech I-Tecar is the gold standard for this modality.

Tecar therapy works both in capacitive mode (for superficial and muscle tissue) and resistive mode (for deep structures like ligaments, tendons, and bone) — making it remarkably versatile.

  • Deep thermal and athermal effects for rapid recovery
  • Stimulates tissue metabolism and oxygenation
  • Effective for both acute injuries and chronic conditions
  • Reduces recovery time significantly post-sport
Sports InjuriesTendinopathyMuscle RecoveryArthritisChronic Pain

The intuitive touchscreen interface and anatomical guide make treatment selection precise and efficient — ideal for outpatient physiotherapy settings where a wide range of conditions need to be addressed.
